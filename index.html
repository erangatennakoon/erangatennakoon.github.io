Understanding Generative AI: An Overview

Generative AI is a type of artificial intelligence designed to create new content, such as text, images, music, or even videos. Unlike traditional AI, which typically analyzes and processes existing data, generative AI can produce original data that mimics human creativity. This remarkable capability has opened up exciting possibilities in various fields, from entertainment to healthcare.
